Campaigns on GOV.UK

Creating accessible and flexible templates

In order to truly be the home of all government services and information we needed to make room within GOV.UK's eco-system for time-boxed marketing style campaign content. Mia Allers and I worked to develop a campaign publishing tool that enabled greater design flexibility in terms of layout options and brand adaptability. Building on the trusted and accessible GOV.UK design language in a sustainable way was key to this work.
