I work for the Government Digital Service (GDS). I employ a reductive approach to my design output and firmly believe in the strength of concise yet sophisticated communication. This comes to life in my pictographic work and also in my work for the UK government presenting information as clearly as possible for the millions of citizens in the UK. My pictographic work has been published by Unit Editions in London. GDS won the Design Museum’s Design of the Year 2013 and a D&AD black (and yellow) pencil, both for GOV.UK.
